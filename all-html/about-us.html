About Us

Susanto Liman

For our founder, coffee has never been just a drink—it’s been a constant companion through every chapter of life. From pulling all-nighters as a student with a mug in hand, to early mornings building a business plan at a tiny kitchen table, coffee has always been there. Not just for the caffeine, but for the ritual, the warmth, the pause between chaos.
After years working in fast-paced corporate environments, he realized what she truly missed: the human connection that only a cup of coffee could offer. Not the instant kind grabbed in a rush, but the slow, thoughtful kind—brewed with care, shared with purpose.
Today, as CEO and founder, he still starts every morning the same way: with a fresh cup, a deep breath, and the belief that something meaningful can begin with something as simple as coffee.

Our Vision

To bring people together through the simple, meaningful experience of coffee—connecting stories, cultures, and communities one cup at a time.

Our Missions

Craft with Purpose – To source and roast high-quality coffee with integrity, care, and consistency.
Empower Communities – To support local farmers through fair and transparent practices.
Slow Moments – To encourage mindful living through coffee experiences.
Innovate Authentically – To continuously explore creative blends, brewing methods, and sustainable practices.
Build Real Connection – To create spaces—physical or digital

Our Story

This journey began not in a café, but in a quiet kitchen with the aroma of freshly brewed coffee filling the air. Our founder wasn’t a barista or a coffee expert—just someone deeply moved by the calm, grounding ritual of making coffee every morning. It wasn’t long before that personal passion grew into a bigger dream: to build a brand that honors the stories and connections behind every cup. Starting small in 2021, with a few beans, a borrowed roaster, and a lot of late nights, we set out to create more than just great coffee. We wanted to build something honest. Something that respects where the coffee comes from, and who it’s shared with.

From working directly with local farmers to experimenting with unique blends and brewing styles, every part of our process is guided by intention. We believe coffee is more than just a product—it’s a moment, a memory, a conversation starter. Today, we continue that journey with our community—one cup at a time.
