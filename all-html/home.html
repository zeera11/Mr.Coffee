The Story Behind the Cup

For our founder, Susanto Liman, coffee has always meant more than caffeine. It was there during late-night studies and early startup mornings—comforting, grounding, and full of quiet purpose. After years in corporate life, he craved real connection—the kind found over a thoughtfully brewed cup.

That’s how Mr.Coffee was born: from a daily ritual into a place where coffee creates something meaningful, one cup at a time.
